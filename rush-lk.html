UI/UX Design 2024

Project Overview

UI/UX design for a buffet reservation platform for appiGo, focusing on creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface for restaurant reservations. This project demonstrates expertise in user experience design and modern interface development for the food service industry.

Key Features

  • Intuitive restaurant browsing and search
  • Real-time table availability and booking
  • User-friendly reservation management
  • Restaurant reviews and ratings system
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go booking
  • Payment integration for advance bookings
  • Restaurant owner dashboard and management
  • Push notifications for booking confirmations

Design Process

The design process began with comprehensive user research, including interviews with restaurant owners and customers. User personas were created to understand different user types and their needs. Wireframes and prototypes were developed in Figma, focusing on creating a seamless booking experience that works across all devices.

User Experience Focus

The design prioritizes user experience with clear navigation, intuitive booking flow, and accessible interface elements. Special attention was given to mobile users, as most restaurant bookings happen on mobile devices. The design includes features like quick booking, saved preferences, and easy cancellation options.

Project Information

Client: appiGo
Duration: 1.5 months
Team Size: 1 designer
Role: UI/UX Designer

Design Tools Used

Figma Adobe XD Prototyping Wireframing User Research Design Systems

Challenges & Solutions

Complex Booking Flow

Simplified the reservation process by creating a step-by-step booking flow with clear progress indicators and confirmation steps.

Mobile-First Design

Designed with mobile users in mind, ensuring all features are easily accessible on small screens while maintaining functionality.

Restaurant Owner Needs

Created separate interfaces for restaurant owners to manage bookings, menus, and availability while maintaining simplicity.
